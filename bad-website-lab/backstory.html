Early Life

Arrival at the Monastary

Mysophinies was born in a goblin camp, which was raided before he ever knew his parents. A raiding party of adventures came to defeat and raze the goblin camp. One of the party members couldnt bring himself to kill the baby mysophinies, and knew that leaving him alone in an empty camp would be the same as doing it with his sword. He brought the baby goblin to a nearby monastary of Necros and left him on the doorstep. Mysophines was raised by the monks outside of goblin culture or ideals.
