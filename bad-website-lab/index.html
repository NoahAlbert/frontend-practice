Mysophinies

Mysophinies is a warrior monk of the temple of Necros.

As a warrior monk, he has mastered his own internal energy called ki.

He manipulates his ki and the ki of others to defend his monastary.

As a member of the Way of the Long Death and follower of Necros, Mysophinies views death as a natural and essential process in the cycle of life.
